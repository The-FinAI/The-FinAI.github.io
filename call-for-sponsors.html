Why Sponsor

Financial services are rapidly adopting large language models and advanced AI across investment research, risk management, compliance, legal analysis, regulatory reporting, customer service, market intelligence, and decision support. FinNLP brings these communities together with researchers, students, and policymakers working at the intersection of NLP, machine learning, finance, economics, and law.

By sponsoring FinNLP, organizations support a leading research community while gaining visibility among researchers, students, engineers, financial professionals, legal experts, AI builders, and industry practitioners.

FinNLP @ EMNLP 2026 focuses on the open challenges that matter to industry practitioners:

  • Accuracy, reliability, and factuality in high-stakes settings
  • Hallucination and misinformation risks
  • Complex financial documents, tables, charts, and numerical data
  • Regulatory, legal, compliance, privacy, and data protection constraints
  • Domain-specific evaluation, benchmarking, responsible AI, transparency, and governance

Sponsorship Principles

Sponsorship and naming recognition are intended to support FinNLP, FinEval, and the broader financial NLP and financial AI community. Award selection remains fully independent and is determined by the workshop organizers, program committee, award committee, or challenge organizers, as appropriate.

Sponsorship does not provide
  • Influence over paper review or acceptance decisions
  • Access to confidential submissions, reviews, reviewer identities, or author identities during review
  • Preferential treatment for sponsor-affiliated submissions
  • Control over award selection or challenge outcomes
  • Control over workshop content, speaker selection, or program decisions
  • Endorsement of sponsor products or services by ACL, EMNLP, SIG-FinTech, FinNLP, FinEval, or The Fin AI

All sponsorship arrangements must comply with applicable ACL, EMNLP, SIG-FinTech, and workshop policies, as well as conflict-of-interest, academic integrity, privacy, and responsible AI principles.
