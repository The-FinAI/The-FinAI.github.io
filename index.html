[ 01 ] // ABOUT

Interdisciplinary venue
for financial AI.

The FinNLP workshop explores the intersection of Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning, and Large Language Models within the financial, economics, and legal domains. The goal is to foster interdisciplinary research and innovation, addressing the multifaceted challenges inherent in these fields.

Since its inception in 2019, FinNLP has been the annual workshop of the ACL Special Interest Group on Financial and Economic NLP (SIG-FinTech). By co-locating with EMNLP, IJCAI, IJCNLP-AACL, LREC, and COLING, FinNLP has bridged the AI and NLP research communities. All proceedings are openly archived in the ACL Anthology.

FinNLP 2026 is the 11th edition of the workshop, co-located with EMNLP 2026 in Budapest, Hungary.
