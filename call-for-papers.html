// For Researchers // EMNLP 2026 · Budapest

Call for Papers_

Submit your research at the intersection of natural language processing, machine learning, and finance. Published in the ACL Anthology.

[ 01 ] // SCOPE

What we'relooking for.

FinNLP 2026 invites submissions exploring the intersection of NLP, machine learning, and large language models within the financial, economics, and legal domains. The workshop is co-located with EMNLP 2026 in Budapest, Hungary (October 24–29, 2026).

We welcome contributions addressing the unique characteristics of financial documents — tabular and numerical data, long-form documents, multi-modal content — as well as legal and compliance challenges in the financial industry.

Topics

  • Language modeling on financial corpora
  • Graph representation learning on financial data
  • Financial search and question answering systems
  • Event discovery on organizational equity price
  • ESG event discovery, evaluation, and impact assessment
  • Compliance monitoring · Financial auditing · Regulatory reporting
  • Ethics, bias mitigation, privacy, data protection
  • Responsible AI practices and governance
  • LLMs in finance and law — methods, metrics, benchmarks, datasets
[ 02 ] // FORMATS

Submissionguidelines.

Long papers

Up to 8 pages of content, plus unlimited references. Suited to substantive original research with thorough evaluation.

Short / demo papers

Up to 4 pages of content, plus unlimited references. Focused contributions, novel ideas, negative results, or system demonstrations.

Formatting & review

Submissions must follow the official ACL style templates and be submitted as anonymized PDFs. Accepted papers are published in the ACL Anthology.
